Our Story

Passion Meets
Precision

Founded in 1999 in the heart of the UAE, Ginyard International Co. was built on a single conviction — that every driver deserves a car that matches their personality and ambition. From day one, we set out to bring the world's most thrilling sports cars to those who truly appreciate them.

Over more than two decades, we have refined our approach to automotive retail. We don't simply sell cars; we listen carefully to each customer's taste, lifestyle, and vision, then guide them to the model that becomes an extension of who they are.

"Our goal has always been to understand your taste — and then exceed your expectations."

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Three pillars that have guided every decision since 1999.

Customer First

We invest time to understand every client's unique taste and driving style before recommending a single model. Your satisfaction is our starting point, not our finish line.

Uncompromised Quality

Every vehicle in our showroom passes a rigorous inspection process. We partner exclusively with trusted manufacturers to guarantee authenticity and peak performance.

Lasting Relationships

We don't close deals — we open relationships. Our team stays with you long after the sale, providing ongoing support, advice, and access to the latest arrivals.
